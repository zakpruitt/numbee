How To Play
Guess the daily bee that adds up to the daily num in 5 tries or less.

Each guess must be a valid 6 letter word. Hit the checkmark button to submit your guess.

After a guess, the color of the tiles will change to reflect the correctness of your guess.

The goal of the game is to build a word, or a bee, that adds up to the daily number, or num.

Number Scale
Each consonant follows a numerical order, while vowels are ALWAYS worth 1.

For example, the word ATTACK is worth 1 + 17 + 17 + 1 + 3 + 9 = 40.
